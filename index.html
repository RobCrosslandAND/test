In recent news, the Black Lives Matter movement has been gaining momentum and coverage due the unfortunate and unlawful killing of George Floyd. The black community however have been facing injustices in their daily lives for as long as we can remember, and it is a fight for Justice that has been taking place long before today. There are countless amounts of black people who have made great contributions to society as a whole and the fight for equality and justice!
